Top Visitor Countries

China emerged as the dominant force in Singapore's tourism industry by 2024, driven by its large population and growing outbound tourism market. Indonesia maintained a strong second place due to its proximity and cultural ties to Singapore. Other regional players like India, Malaysia, and the Philippines have shown steady growth, while Western nations like Australia and the USA remain important but less dominant.

Tourism Spots

Notable tourism clusters are concentrated in areas such as Marina Bay, Sentosa Island, and Orchard Road, reflecting their status as popular tourist hubs. The distribution of tourism spots shows Singapore's ability to offer diverse experiences, from urban and commercial hotspots to nature reserves and cultural sites spread throughout the city-state.
